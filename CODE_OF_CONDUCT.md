## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a friendly, safe, and inclusive environment for all contributors to the Thaumiel Map Editor project. This Code of Conduct outlines the standards of behavior expected from everyone involved in the project, whether as developers, testers, or community members.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including but not limited to:
- The official repository
- Communication channels (e.g., Discord, GitHub Discussions, forums)

## Expected Behavior

- **Respectful Communication**: Treat others with kindness and respect, even during disagreements. Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language.
- **Collaborative Spirit**: Share knowledge, provide constructive feedback, and prioritize the project's goals over individual interests.
- **Inclusivity**: Welcome diverse perspectives and backgrounds. Avoid assumptions about others' experiences or expertise.
- **Professionalism**: Maintain a focus on problem-solving and project progress. Avoid unproductive or disruptive behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind
- Hate speech, racism, sexism, or other discriminatory remarks
- Personal attacks or insults
- Spamming, trolling, or intentionally derailing discussions
- Violating the project's licensing or intellectual property rights

## Enforcing the Code

Project maintainers have the authority to address violations. Actions may include:
- Warning or temporarily suspending individuals
- Removing content or banning users

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers in our Discord guild [Discord invite](https://discord.gg/N8qrNHf4s9). All reports will be handled confidentially and promptly.

We appreciate your help in keeping the Thaumiel Map Editor community welcoming for everyone.
